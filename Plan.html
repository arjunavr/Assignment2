Mission Statement:
The main goal of this website is to inspire the kids of the world and spread the latest lifestyle fashion to them. It is also a mission to provide the kids with the best quality and most comfortable clothes for all the kids.

Success Evaluation:
We can evaluate the success by:

  • Performing usability test on the targeted audience.
  • Evaluate and analyze the problems encountered by the users while attending the usability test.
  • Plan and rectify the problems faced by the end users.
  • The end prototype of the website is released and the number of visitors and customers are noted.

Target Audience
The main target audiences are kids of all ages and their parents. Kid’s attention are drawn more to colors. The website is very colorful and pleasing for the kids to see. These generation kids are more accessible to mobile devices and our website is compatible with mobile devices. Kids and parents will just have to access the website and choose the latest fashion trends that are out in the market and pay for the product and delivery
